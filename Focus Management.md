
## 1. What is Deep Work?
Focusing without distractions and producing high-quality work.

## 2. According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
- Eliminate Distractions
- Work in Time-Intervals
- Have a Routine
- Have Work Goals
- Take Rest and Refresh

## 3. How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
- By Planning our day
- Self-Introspection
- Having Clear Goals
- Celebrating Our Work
- Working in Time Blocks

## 4. What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
- **Time Wastage**: Infinite scrolling consumes valuable time.
- **Addiction**: Reduces productivity and focus
- You may feel **Isolated** from real-world interactions.
- **Reduces Our Intensity Level**
- **Mental Health Issues** : Comparison and negative interactions can cause depression, anxiety and low self-esteem.
